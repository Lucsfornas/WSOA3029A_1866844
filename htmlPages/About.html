About Me



Hi, I am Luca Fornasari, a Digital Arts Student studying at the University of Witwaterstrand.


This is my Project website for Interactive Media 3, WSOA3029A. The theme for our second semester project is the visualistation of data on the Web. In this website I will be making use of a Covid-19 API where i use that data to create graphs to better visualize the data.


In todays modern world there is unfathomable ammounts of data being sent across the web every minute, with such vast ammounts of data humanity could never even dream of analising it all by themselves, this is where data visualisation comes into play. By using visual ways such as charts to plot this data, everyday people have easy access to the data and an easy understanding of what that data represents. This visualisation of data is what will allow our species as a whole to become smarter and develope quicker. With nearly everyone in the world having access to a smartphone data visualisation becomes even more important, providing people even in remote locations of the world with access to information by the touch of a button.



Style Sheet



While designing the website's wireframe and the colours used the fact that around 300 million people in the world are colour blind became apparent. The design of the site was specific towards this, with the nav bar being in a solid close to black colour with white text, this makes the text easily readable if someone is colour blind. The body of the website is also designed in this way, with the background of the body being a light red colour with dark blue text, this makes it easier to read for a colourblind person because of the difference in the light and dark text making it easily identifyable.


The colours of the website that were chosen are vibrant reds for the background of the website, these red colours fade into eachother making the background interesting, this vibrant background mixed with the dark blue writing makes the site easily readable. The design of the nav bar differs with a dark close to black background with white writing for the logo of the website and the nav link elements. For sections of the website that have large ammounts of text on it i have added a border around the paragraphs of text which helps with the reader easily keep track and i have found that it reduces the ammount to times that my eyes skip words.
